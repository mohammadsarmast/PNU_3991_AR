In summary, there are a variety of Web-enabled collaborative applications that are capable of supporting an expanding list of group activities. It seems likely that these applications will beeome an increasingly important and valuable component of the e-research process. It is also likely that these applications will become more useful, as additional features are added and their functionality increased through user feed- back. We also expect that increasing access to Net services provided by mobile devices will increase the ease and practicality of collaboration research.
